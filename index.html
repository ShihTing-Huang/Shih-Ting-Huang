Hi, I am Shih-Ting Huang

About Men

I am a Ph.D candidate at Ruhr University, Bochum, Germany now working in the field of high dimensional statistics. My research area includes Biological Networks, High-dimensional Theory, Machine Learning and Big Data, and Empirical Processes. One of my research project is to obtain a novel method for calibration of tuning parameter in ridge regression for personalized medicine which is done on September, 2019. My current project is related to a tuning-free regression for high-dimensional statistics. Before I came here to Germany, I worked as a data scientist in Yulon Motor in Taiwan. My job was constructing a statistical model for tire-wear prediction and help the customer service for preventive maintenance. At 2017, I was a Ph.D student at KAUST in Saudi Arabia working in the research group of environmental statistics. Basically, I studied the wavelet analysis of time series data for evolving periodic function. I got my master's degree at Temple University, USA and my research there was related to the quantum computing. Specifically, I derived the quantum version of Levy stable process that has the sub-ballistic behavior.
